About Us

ByteJam 2024 Project

A news website with the ability to go back in time to different decades, each featuring their own significant stories, supported by New York Times Archive API (hence the similar logo).
Featuring the decades of the 2020s, 2010s, 2000s, and the 1990s.

Our Mission

To inform the public, about archival information. That and also feature this website :)

Resources

Built with HTML/CSS, Python, Flask, and Jinja

The Process

We began with wireframe planning and design choices, before splitting into front-end and back-end to complete our assigned tasks
